The Service Provided

Background Information

My name is Paul How and I have owned this Rolls Royce since 2005. I have offered wedding car hire within the county of Ceredigion for over seven years. During this time I have driven many brides, grooms, bridesmaids and guests to their destinations. It is a very personal service, from initially meeting the bride and groom when arrangements are discussed, to carrying out their wishes to the exact detail on their special day. I remain very flexible and if possible make picturesque detours on request or vary the itinerary if required. I am always ready to oblige and do everything possible to make the experience an unforgettable part of the day.

On Your Wedding Day

Each wedding is different and requires a different approach which I fully understand. The service is not a "taxi"; it's much more than that. The car and service is available from 2 hours before the wedding which can be used for multiple journeys if the timings allow. Usually the car remains at the reception until the couple and guests enter for their meal. This allows for photo opportunities with the Rolls Royce, using the car for unique compositions for both the wedding party and guests.

High Standard

I will only undertake one wedding on each day to ensure total dedication to the event. The car is always highly polished and kept immaculate throughout the day. I operate within the county of Ceredigion and borders and I am based at Aberaeron in West Wales. All income is divulged and I conform to the required vehicle insurance for this operation.
